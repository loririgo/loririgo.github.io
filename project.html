Pup up! is more than just a medical facility—it's a place of hope and healing for animals in need. We understand that every animal deserves a second chance, and our dedicated clinic team works tirelessly to provide the best possible care to ensure each one gets it.

On-site clinic offers comprehensive veterinary services, including wellness exams, vaccinations, spaying and neutering, dental care, and emergency treatments. From the moment an animal arrives at our shelter, our compassionate veterinarians and skilled support staff are committed to their health and well-being. We carefully assess each animal's needs, develop personalized care plans, and provide ongoing medical support as they prepare for adoption.

Our clinic plays a vital role in the mission of Pup Up!: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals. With modern equipment and a team that truly cares, we are able to address a wide range of medical needs - from routine check-ups to complex surgeries - ensuring that every animal receives the care they deserve.

At Pup Up!, we believe that every life is precious. Our clinic is where the journey to a brighter future begins for many of the animals we serve. Thank you for supporting our mission and helping us give every animal the chance to live a happy, healthy life.
